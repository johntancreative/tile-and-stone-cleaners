What We Do

Core Services

Professional cleaning, sealing, and restoration for every tile and stone surface.

Tile & Grout Cleaning

Deep extraction cleaning to remove built-up grime, grease, and bacteria from tile and grout lines.

Learn more →

Cleaning & Sealing

Professional cleaning followed by protective sealant application for long-lasting surface protection.

Learn more →

Stripping & Sealing

Complete removal of old sealant and coatings, followed by fresh sealer application for a renewed finish.

Learn more →

Stone Honing

Diamond abrasive honing to achieve a smooth, matte finish that removes scratches and wear marks.

Learn more →

Stone Polishing

Multi-stage diamond polishing to restore a high-gloss, mirror-like finish to natural stone surfaces.

Learn more →

High-Pressure Cleaning

Industrial-strength pressure washing to remove dirt, algae, mould, and moss from outdoor surfaces.

Learn more →

Efflorescence Treatment

Removal of white mineral salt deposits that form on stone and tile surfaces due to moisture migration.

Learn more →

Mould Treatment

Professional mould removal and treatment to eliminate spores and prevent regrowth on tile and grout.

Learn more →
