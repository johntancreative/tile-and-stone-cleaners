Professional High-Pressure Cleaning in Sydney

Industrial-strength pressure washing removes dirt, algae, mould, and moss from outdoor surfaces. We adjust pressure settings for each surface type to deliver maximum cleaning power without causing damage -- restoring your outdoor areas to like-new condition.

What We Clean

  • Driveways and carports
  • Patios and entertaining areas
  • Pool surrounds and decking
  • Pathways and walkways
  • Retaining walls
  • Stairs and steps
  • Garage floors
  • Building exteriors

Our Approach

  • Adjustable pressure from 800 to 3000 PSI depending on surface
  • Hot water extraction for grease and oil stains
  • Pre-treatment chemicals for mould, algae, and biological growth
  • Careful technique on delicate surfaces to prevent damage
  • Surface-specific nozzles and fan patterns

Surface-Specific Pressure Guide

Delicate Surfaces

Sandstone and limestone: max 800 PSI. Terracotta: max 1100 PSI. These softer materials require lower pressure to avoid surface erosion and pitting.

Hard Surfaces

Bluestone and granite: up to 2000 PSI. Concrete and pavers: up to 3000 PSI. These dense materials can handle higher pressure for deeper cleaning results.

Pair with Sealing

Pressure cleaning removes the old buildup -- sealing protects the new, clean surface. We strongly recommend sealing immediately after pressure cleaning while the surface is at its cleanest. This combination delivers the longest-lasting results and prevents rapid re-soiling.
