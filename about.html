Our Story

Premium Quality Work at Affordable Prices

At Tile & Stone Cleaners, we deliver premium quality work at affordable prices. We believe every home deserves freshly cleaned and restored floors, walls and pathways.

With over 15 years of experience servicing properties across 9 Sydney regions, we've built our reputation on consistent results and genuine care for our customers' homes.

We work all year round to exceed the expectations of our customers through our values. Every project, whether it's a single bathroom or an entire commercial property, receives the same level of attention and professionalism.
